Snow Sculpture Winter Fest

The first Snow Sculpture Winter Festival in Manitoba was held in Brandon in 1990, and was held there annually until 2015 when it moved to Brandon in 2000, and has been held there since.

The SS Winter Fest of Manitoba as far as we have been able to determine, is the second oldest continuous ethnic festival in North America.
